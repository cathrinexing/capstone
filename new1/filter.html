Find My Program

Thirty-three UX related graduate programs are identified in the U.S. Based on the interviews from professionals, jobs in user experience can divided into 2 categories: design and research. All programs listed are suitable for designers, while 22 of 33 are also suitable for a career in research. Moreover, the programs are ranked in descending order in terms of the number of courses that professionals consider useful at work.

According to the interviews from students, the factors they consider important are tuition, campus setting, program length and institute type.

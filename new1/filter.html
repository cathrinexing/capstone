Find Your Programs

Thirty-three UX-related graduate Programs are identified in the U.S. Based on the interviews from professionals, careers can divided into 2 categories: UX designer and UX researcher. 33 Programs are suitable for UX designer, while 22 of 33 are suitable for UX researcher. Moreover, the programs are ranked with descending order in terms of the number of courses that professionals consider useful at work.

According to the interviews from students about the factors they consider important when choosing programs, and their rankings of the factor, I selected the most important factors, including tuition, campus setting, program length and institute type.

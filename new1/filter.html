Find My Program

Thirty-three UX related graduate programs are identified in the U.S. Based on interviews from professionals, careers in user experience can be mainly divided into 2 categories: designer and researcher. All programs listed are suitable for designers, while 22 of 33 are also suitable for a career in research. Moreover, programs are ranked in a descending order in terms of the number of courses that UX professionals consider useful at work.

According to the interviews from students, the factors they consider important are tuition, campus setting, program length and institute type.

Programs overview

According to interviews from students, they would love to see a general overview of all programs in terms of tuition, location, major type and Degree type.

Tuition Overview

For outstate tuition, most programs will cost about 40,000 to 60,000 dollars. Each dot represent a program, hover over to see detailed information.

Location Overview

Most programs are offered along the Western coast and Northeastern regions. Each dot represent a program, hover over to see detailed information.

Major Overview

All programs can be framed into four major field, including Design/Media, Human Computer Interaction, Information and Human Factor.

Degree Overview

Nine degree types are offered in these 33 UX-related programs. The majority degree types are MS, MPS and MFA.
